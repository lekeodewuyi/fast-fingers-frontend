About

Fast fingers is a fun app for practicing typing. The instruction is simple - reproduce the "sample text" exactly as it is. There are visual cues to notify you of errors.

You get a timer that starts once you begin typing - you can pause the timer and resume any time and as many times as you need to during the practice session.

Once you successfully complete the practice session, you get a session statistics to see how well you performed.
